About Us

Beauty that empowers. Confidence that shines.

Brand Story

Roxanne Glam Cosmetics was founded with a passion for beauty and a mission to empower individuals through high quality, affordable cosmetics. Since our launch in 2020, we have been dedicated to offering skincare and makeup products that help everyone feel confident and beautiful.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To enhance natural beauty and promote confidence through affordable, high quality cosmetics.

Our Vision

To be a leading cosmetic brand that inspires self-love and celebrates diversity.

About the Founder

Roxanne Johnson

Founder & CEO

Roxanne's love for beauty started at a young age. She dreamed of creating a brand that makes high quality cosmetics accessible to everyone. Today, Roxanne Glam is a reflection of that dream and her commitment to empowering others.
